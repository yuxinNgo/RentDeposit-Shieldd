Problem02 / 12

Deposit money moves. Trust does not.

Tenant

Needs proof that a deposit is protected and a clear path to a refund.

Landlord

Needs a reliable record of move-in evidence and settlement status.

Mediator

Needs the same evidence trail when a dispute requires a decision.

Today, deposits, messages, photos, and payment references live in different places. That makes a simple rental workflow hard to verify.
